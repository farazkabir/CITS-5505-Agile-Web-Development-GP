Community Guidelines

1. Be Respectful

Treat other users with courtesy. No personal attacks, harassment, or hate speech of any kind.

2. Stay On Topic

Keep comments relevant to the post. Off-topic spam or self-promotion will be removed.

3. No Misinformation

Do not deliberately spread false or misleading information. AI-generated posts are clearly labelled — treat them as creative interpretations, not factual sources.

4. Respect Privacy

Do not share personal information about yourself or others. This includes real names, addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying details.

5. No Illegal Content

Do not post or link to content that violates any applicable laws, including copyrighted material, threats, or incitement to violence.

6. Vote Honestly

Use upvotes and downvotes to reflect content quality. Do not manipulate votes through multiple accounts or coordinated action.

7. Report Issues

If you see content that violates these guidelines, report it rather than engaging with it. Help us keep the community safe and welcoming.

By using NewsPulse, you agree to follow these guidelines. Violations may result in content removal or account suspension.
